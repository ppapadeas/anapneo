NEOs

A near-Earth object (NEO) is a Solar System object whose orbit brings it into proximity with the Earth.

All NEOs have a closest approach to the Sun (perihelion) of less than 1.3 AU. They include a few thousand near-Earth asteroids (NEAs), near-Earth comets, a number of solar-orbiting spacecraft, and meteoroids large enough to be tracked in space before striking the Earth.
